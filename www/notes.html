Notes:
In the Ecosystem Type Change Matrix:
  • The diagonal values are the amounts unchanged for that group.
  • Each row is the unchanged areas plus the reduction in area.
  • Each column is the unchanged areas plus the additions in area.
  • The sum of a row will equal the opening extent.
  • The sum of a column will equal the closing extent.
For multiple time points:
  • The (n) time points are assumed to be in chronological order.
  • Opening (1) being the first time point and Closing (n) being the last time point.
  • The n-1 extent accounts tackle periods i-1 to i for i=2,...,n
